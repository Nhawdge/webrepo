Server Commands

Teleportation

/tpa player

This will prompt player to accept a teleportation request

/tpahere player

This will request player to accept a teleportation request to you

/tpaccept

This will accept the TPA request from another player

/rtp

This will randomly teleport you around the world

/sethome [home]

This will assign home to your current location and facing.

/home [home]

This will teleport you home Note that home is only required if you have more than one home set.

/back

This will teleport you back to your last teleport location

/warp [warp]

This will teleport you to warp.

Claims

/Claimlist

Shows total amount of claim blocks available, and current claims

/AbandonClaim

This will abandon the claim you are currently standing in.

/Trust player

Gives build trust to player to your claim. If you are not standing on a claim this will trust them to all claims.

/permissiontrust player

Gives permission trust to player to your claim. This allows that player to give trust to your claim. If you are not standing on a claim this will trust them to all claims.

/containertrust player

Gives container trust to player to your claim. Player can now access your chests, without being able to build. If you are not standing on a claim this will trust them to all claims.

/accesstrust player

Gives access trust to player to your claim. If you are not standing on a claim this will trust them to all claims. manage build containers access

Mail

/mail send player text

This will send player some mail containing text.

/mail read

This command will show any mail you have received

/mail clear

This will clear your inbox

Misc Perks

/fly

This will let you activate fly mode. Press space bar twice to activate

/craft

This opens a portable crafting table

/echest

This gives you access to your ender chest.
