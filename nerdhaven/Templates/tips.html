Gamma Trick

Open the minecraft settings file located at C:\Users\{Yournamehere}\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\. Or in the minecraft options menu, open resource pack folder and go up one level.

You must close minecraft entirely before making this change, or it will be overwritten.

In the Options.txt file, set the gamma to 1000.0. The line should look like gamma:1000.0
